Exploring Europe through images, sound and media

📸 Travel Photo

JPG format: compressed image, good quality with smaller file size. Ideal for photography.
HDR image (hdr.jpg): High Dynamic Range allows better light and color details, making photos look more realistic.

🎨 Graphics

WEBP: modern format, smaller size with high quality (used for web performance).
PNG: high quality image with transparency support, ideal for logos.
Graphics formats are useful for design elements and visual communication.

🎞️ Animation

GIF: supports animation, commonly used for short loops and visual effects.

🔊 Sound

MP3: compressed audio format, widely used because it reduces file size while keeping acceptable quality.

🎥 Video

Embedded video from YouTube, which saves space and loads faster than uploading video files.
